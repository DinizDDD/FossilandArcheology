Game Info

Fossils and Archaeology Revival mod (affectionately known as F/A) is a continuation of the original Fossils and Archaeology mod created by Flammarilva. This mod supports both SSP (Survival Single Player) and SMP (Survival Multiplayer) using Forge or Fabric in Minecraft 1.18.2. The newest public version 9.0.0.4 is available for 1.18.2 can be played on both Forge and Fabric and is dependant on Architectury API, More Hitboxes, GeckoLib and Terrablender (Forge) or Terrablender (Fabric) depending which loader you're using.

Paleontology

The Paleontology side of the mod adds a wide assortment of mobs, tools, and cools itens. A great majority of the mobs are prehistoric creatures that the player has to create. Many others are hostile mobs that reside near Anu, the boss mob of the mod.

Paleobotany

Although this mod is known for its prehistoric creatures, it adds several prehistoric plants as well. They make for beautiful decorations at a base or in an enclosure, and some of them can be used for feeding herbivores.

Archeology

Keeping in mind the archeology portion of this mod, there are quite a few ancient artifacts. Some, like the scarab gem, are integral to the mod. Others are useful tools or beautiful decorations.

Others

The Fossils and Archeology Revival mod adds many items and blocks to Minecraft. These items can be crafted by the player or dropped from blocks or mobs. They range from plant and animal items to mysterious relics from ancient civilizations.
